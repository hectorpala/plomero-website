- **DALL·E 3** (via ChatGPT Plus)

- **Leonardo.ai**

## Context of the Business

**Business:** Plomero Culiacán Pro
**Location:** Culiacán, Sinaloa, México
**Services:**
- Emergency plumbing (24/7)
- Leak detection and repair
- Drain cleaning
- Water heater/boiler maintenance

**Brand values:** Professional, trustworthy, fast, local expertise, 15+ years experience

## Prompt Structure Guidelines

Each prompt MUST include:

1. **Subject:** Clear description of the plumbing scene
2. **Setting:** Culiacán/Mexican residential or commercial context
3. **Style:** Photorealistic, professional, clean
4. **Lighting:** Natural, bright, welcoming (avoid dark/gloomy)
5. **Perspective:** Eye-level, slightly above, professional angle
6. **Mood:** Trustworthy, competent, approachable
7. **Technical specs:** 16:9 aspect ratio, high resolution, sharp focus
8. **Avoid:** Stock photo feel, overly staged, unprofessional attire

## Categories to Cover

Generate prompts for these categories (prioritize first 6):

1. **Hero image** - Professional plumber portrait or at work
2. **Leak repair** - Fixing pipe under sink or wall
3. **Drain cleaning** - Using professional tools (snake, hydrojetter)
4. **Boiler maintenance** - Servicing water heater
5. **Emergency service** - Nighttime or urgent repair scenario
6. **Bathroom installation** - Installing toilet, sink, or fixtures
7. **Tool display** - Professional plumbing tools organized
8. **Before/after** - Transformation of plumbing issue
9. **Customer satisfaction** - Happy homeowner with plumber
10. **Tinaco installation** - Tank installation on roof
